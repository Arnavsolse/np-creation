Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum booking duration?
+
The minimum booking duration is 2 hours. This ensures enough time for setup, recording, and a comfortable buffer for re-takes. We recommend 3 hours for interviews with multiple segments.
How long does video editing take?
+
Our standard editing turnaround is 48‚Äì72 hours for a 30‚Äì60 minute episode. Rush delivery (within 24 hours) is available at an additional charge. We'll discuss timelines during booking.
Do you provide a teleprompter or script assistance?
+
Yes! We provide basic script review and can set up a teleprompter app on request. For structured podcasts, we recommend sharing your episode outline with us 24 hours before the shoot so we can prep the team.
Can I come for a studio visit before booking?
+
Absolutely! We encourage a 15-minute studio visit so you can experience the space, check the gear, and feel the studio vibe. Just WhatsApp us to schedule a free walkthrough.
What is the cancellation policy?
+
Cancellations made 48+ hours in advance receive a full refund or reschedule at no charge. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a 50% booking fee. We understand schedules change ‚Äî just communicate with us promptly!
Do you create content in Marathi?
+
Yes! Marathi-language podcasts are our specialty and the core of our brand identity. We also handle Hindi and English productions. Our social media management team writes captions fluently in all three languages.
What formats do you deliver the final video in?
+
We deliver the main episode in 4K MP4 format optimized for YouTube, plus pre-cut Shorts/Reels (vertical 9:16) for social media. Audio-only MP3 files for podcast platforms are also available on request.
